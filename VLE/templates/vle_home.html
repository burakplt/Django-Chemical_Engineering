ChemicalEasy is more of a practical website rather than full of theoretical explanations. Vapor-Liquid equilibrium is one of the most important thermodynamic concepts that is widely used in process/chemical/mechanical engineering. As you already experienced, lots of complex or long calculations must be performed for the thermodynamic methods. Most of them requires using iterative and numerical calculations, which is overwhelming and not practical. In addition to computational difficulties, physical and thermal properties for the chemicals, namely a database should be provided. In the VLE Calculations section you can freely use the following calculators equipped with 400+ chemicals from ChemSep database (Apache license) and powered with Python.
